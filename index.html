✨ Our Story ✨

edUKai was born from the passion of two dedicated educators: Omar Gouda, a newbie frontend engineer, and Mariam Ramadan, a brilliant teacher and lifelong learner.

This platform is more than just a learning hub—it is a heartfelt gift from Omar to Mariam, to honor her incredible hard work in teaching, her persistence in every phase of her life, and her drive to inspire students with meaningful, career-ready skills.

Together, they built edUKai as a community where knowledge is shared, creativity is celebrated, and students are empowered to achieve more. 🌱

Meet Our Founders

Behind edUKai are two passionate educators who believe in knowledge, growth, and guiding students to success.

Omar Gouda

Frontend Developer & Lifelong Learner

Omar Gouda

Omar graduated from the Faculty of Commerce & Business Administration – Helwan University. His journey began with languages: English, German, and Japanese, before discovering a passion for the web’s own languages: HTML, CSS, JavaScript.

With determination, he built his roadmap and turned his dream into reality: creating edUKai. This platform is his gift of knowledge, crafted with love and persistence, to inspire learners everywhere.

Mariam Ramadan

English & Translation Mentor

Hover to read her story →

Mariam Ramadan

Mariam graduated from the Faculty of Arts – English Section, Kafr El-Sheikh University. She served as a Teaching Assistant at the Arab Academy for Science, Technology & Maritime Transport, earning the admiration of colleagues and students alike.

Though young, her presence commands respect. With patience, care, and passion, she helps learners become fluent English speakers and skilled interpreters. Mariam continues to learn and grow, giving her students more than just lessons—she gives them confidence and inspiration.
