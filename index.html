About edUKai

edUKai was founded by educators Omar Gouda & Mariam Ramadan to share practical, career-ready skills with students nationwide.

Frontend Developer & Instructor. Omar teaches modern HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React through hands-on projects and real-world examples.

English & Translation Mentor. Mariam helps learners improve communication, writing, and translation skills with personalized guidance.
